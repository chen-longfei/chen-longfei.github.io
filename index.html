About Me

I am currently a Visitor at the School of Informatics, University of Edinburgh. Previously, I was a Research Associate at the Advanced Care Research Centre (ACRC). I hold a PhD in Electrical Engineering from Kyoto University (2020).

My research sits at the intersection of Computer Vision and Healthcare. I specialize in Egocentric Vision, Temporal Modeling, and Human Behavior Analysis to develop privacy-preserving AI systems for monitoring the health and independence of older adults.
